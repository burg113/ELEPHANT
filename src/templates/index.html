What is {{APP_NAME}}?

{{APP_NAME}} is a slow and secure proxy for online calendars. With {{APP_NAME}} it's possible to customise online calendars where only have read access.

How does {{APP_NAME}} work?

Every time a calendar app makes a request, our servers request the calendar from the original location. After this we perform the specified transformations on the contents of the calender. So after all you get a cleaner, better and customised calendar.
